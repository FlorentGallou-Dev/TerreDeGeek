COOKIE POLICY

When you visit our shopforgeek.be website, cookies are placed on your computer, mobile or tablet.
Our website is designed to meet the needs and expectations of our visitors. One of the reasons we use cookies is, for example, to allow you to log in and access your account.
This page is here to help you find out more about how cookies work and how to modify their settings.

DELIVERY

These cookies are required for visiting our website or the use of certain parts thereof.
They enable you to:
navigate between the various sections of the website
fill out forms
securely verify your identity before granting access to your personal data upon creating a personal account.
If you refuse the use of cookies, certain sections of the website will be altered or stopped.

PROTECTION OF PERSONAL DATA

The data collected within the framework of an online sale is required to take into account your delivery.
This data is solely used and processed by ShopForGeek.com for the purposes of managing your order and delivering your goods
In accordance with European law on the protection of personal data, you will have a right to access, modify, rectify or delete your personal data. If you would like to exercise said right, you may write to the consumer service of MB Distribution SPRL, 13 avenue Pasteur, 1300 Belgium, or send an email to client@shopforgeek.com

DELIVERY

Orders for available items are normally expedited within 24/48 hours on a workday* (Belgium, Luxembourg) and within 72 hours on workday* in France.
When you place your order, you can request your order to be delivered to an alternative address (work, parents, etc.) at no extra cost.
All deliveries are handled by post. (https://www.bpost.be)

On shopforgeek.com, we offer a wide range of payment methods. You can pay by Visa, MasterCard, Carte Bleu, PayPal, Bancontact, etc.
For all your payments, the transaction is secured by SSL encryption technology. All your payment details are encrypted from your computer to the bank’s server. This information remains confidential and cannot be seen or used without your consent. Payments are carried out via the ATOS WORLDLINE platform
